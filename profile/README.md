Welcome to CapCheck Foundation - A nonprofit dedicated to media literacy education and fact-checking research.

## What We Do

CapCheck Foundation advances public understanding of information verification through education, research, and open-source tools. We develop the methodologies and infrastructure that make instant content verification accessible to everyone.

**Our Mission:** Educate the public on media literacy and advance research in automated fact-checking.

**Our Structure:** Nonprofit owning both capcheck.org and capcheck.ai entities to ensure research independence.
